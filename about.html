Message from our CEO
E & S garment was founded in 2020,in Mekele industrial park with the dream of serving to the apparel sector. I’ve more than 10 years of experience; our company has become a breath of fresh air and driving force for the industry with both its innovative perspective and sustainable policies. Since the first day of establishment we interfuse all of our excitement into the fabrics and reflect the spirit of this excitement within our products which we offer as ready-made clothing.

Mission

E & S Garment is committed to the consistent upliftment and improvement of both its products and its responsibilities towards its employees. Striving hard to achieve the highest levels of customer satisfaction through timely deliveries along with enhanced quality and value of all our products

Vision

At E & S Garment, with well-defined quality objectives in mind to attain customer satisfaction, is at the heart of our business policy and strategy. E & S Garment is committed to manufacturing, source, supply, export, and trade quality textile products and services

Value

As a company policy, we believe in living and practicing internationally approved business ethics and norms that distinguish E & S Garment from its counterparts. Values – honesty, integrity, and human dignity, besides ethical approach in engagements with all connected stakeholders; customers, suppliers, employees, and others.

About

E & S Garment is a family-owned ethical garment manufacturer based in Ethiopia, Mekelle. We provide a full range of cutting, sewing, and finishing services including accessories and embroidery. We currently employ more than 100 people and ship products around the world. We specialize in t-shirts, uniforms, and fashion items for youth and adults.
